Privacy Policy

Information about the data we collect and how we use it

🛡️ Your privacy is our priority. RT Bot only collects the minimum data needed to operate the games point features and never sells your data to third parties.
1
Data We Collect

RT Bot automatically collects the following data when you interact with it:

  • Discord User ID — Your unique account identifier (not your name or email)
  • Server (Guild) ID — To separate point data between servers
  • Point Balance — Your current points and transaction history
  • Game History — Win/loss logs, challenges, and bot activity
  • Activity Timestamps — Time of point transactions for fraud prevention
⚠️ We do NOT collect: full names, email addresses, phone numbers, Discord passwords, or the content of your private messages.
2
How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Running the points system and leaderboard accurately
  • Detecting and preventing cheating or exploitation
  • Recovering data in the event of a technical error
  • Improving bot performance and features
  • Enforcing the Terms of Service
3
Data Storage

Your data is stored on our secure servers. Security measures we apply include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Database access restricted to the development team only
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss
  • No storage of user Discord passwords or tokens
📦 Data is retained while the account or server is actively using RT Bot. Data inactive for more than 180 days may be automatically deleted.
4
Sharing Data with Third Parties

RT Bot does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or a valid court order
  • To prevent serious security threats
  • Infrastructure services we use (hosting, database) under confidentiality agreements
5
Your Data Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Data Access — View the data we store about you via bot commands
  • Data Deletion — Request deletion of all your data (account reset)
  • Data Correction — Report any incorrect data to be fixed
  • Data Portability — Request an export of your point data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the official Discord server.

6
Children's Privacy

RT Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13, in accordance with Discord's policy. If we become aware that a user is under 13 years old, their data will be promptly deleted and bot access will be restricted.

🔞 If you know of a user under 13 years old using this bot, please report it to our team immediately.
7
Cookies & Tracking Technologies

RT Bot operates entirely within the Discord platform and does not use cookies or web-based tracking technologies. This documentation page (which you are currently reading) also does not use any third-party analytics services.

8
Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be:

  • Announced on the official Discord server at least 7 days before taking effect
  • Reflected in an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Communicated via bot broadcast message (if the feature is available)
9
Contact Us

Questions regarding your data privacy can be submitted via:

  • The #privacy-support channel in the official RT Bot Discord server
  • Direct message to the RT Bot Developer

We are committed to responding to privacy requests within 72 business hours.

🔐 We are committed to keeping your data safe and private. Your trust is the foundation of our service.
